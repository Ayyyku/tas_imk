Location

The closest major cities to Borobudur with international flights are Semarang and Yogyakarta. These two cities accommodate flight routes from Kuala Lumpur and Singapore, but Yogyakarta (with a distance of 40 km from Borobudur) is closer than Semarang (with a distance of 90 km). The other closest city to Borobudur is Magelang with a distance of 17 km, which has a direct bus route to Borobudur.

Bus from Yogyakarta
From Yogyakarta, you need to go to the Jombor bus terminal to take a bus bound for Borobudur. If you are in the city, you can take the Trans Jogja bus to Jombor terminal. Buses to Borobudur are usually smaller than inter-city buses between provinces and cost around IDR 10,000. Travel time is usually around 1 hour. After arriving at the Borobudur terminal, you can continue your journey by pedicab, train or on foot. Private Vehicle from Yogyakarta
The main road to Borobudur from Yogyakarta is via Jalan Magelang. To get to Magelang road, you can go to the Jombor bus terminal. From Jombor move north to Magelang City. After approximately 40 km, you will find the Muntilan Sharp Bamboo Monument. Not long after the monument, you will meet the Palbapang (Mungkid Magelang) T-junction, take the left direction. The distance from Palbapang to Borobudur is around 10 km. Mendut Temple is approximately 4.5 km away.

Bus From Semarang
From the Semarang bus terminal, take a bus bound for Jogja / Magelang.You can get off at several places such as the Magelang Bus Terminal, or the Blondo intersection (junction between Mertoyudan and Blabak), or the Palbapang intersection (junction between Blabak and Muntilan). From there you can continue your journey to the Borobudur terminal by using the bus towards Borobudur. The distance from Semarang to Borobudur is around 90 km, travel time is around 3.5 hours
